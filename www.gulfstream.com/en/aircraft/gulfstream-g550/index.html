INGENUITY IN FLIGHT

The G550 continues to impress with the iconic beauty and uncompromising performance that helped define modern business aviation. High-thrust Rolls-Royce engines and an outstanding maximum payload allow it to carry more of your essentials for greater distances with a low fuel burn. First introduced on the G550, the PlaneView™ flight deck remains an industry leader in safety.

EARNED THE 2003 ROBERT J. COLLIER TROPHY

LEADERSHIP QUALITY

The G550 was designed to excel under pressure, consistently displaying its superior dispatch reliability. Modular avionics units reduce avionics box count and are easily upgraded. Throughout the flight deck, built-in redundancies add an additional layer of safety for everyone onboard. Authorized pilots may rely solely upon our Enhanced Flight Vision System (EFVS) to land, increasing access to airports.

VETERAN INNOVATOR

The G550 pioneered several performance capabilities and flight deck innovations. The award-winning PlaneView avionics suite advanced business aviation with an integrated glass flight deck. Optimized 14-in/36-cm LCD displays stretch across the control center, increasing viewing area while decreasing pilot workload with streamlined flight data.

THE AIRCRAFT OF CHOICE FOR SPECIAL MISSIONS WORLDWIDE

SPECIAL MISSIONS

Due to its flexibility, capability and signature dispatch reliability, the G550 is the aircraft of choice for a wide range of missions. It leads the way in business, government and military special missions—including aeromedical evacuation, airborne early warning, ground surveillance, head-of-state transport and maritime patrol.
